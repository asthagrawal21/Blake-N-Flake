Cake Recipe

Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  1. 3 eggs

    2.

  2. 100g castor sugar

    3.

  3. 85g self raising flour

    4.

Notes on Ingredients

Castor sugar-Finely granulated white sugar

Self-Raising flour-A pre-mixed combination of flour

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degree C.

    2.

  2. Greace a 20 cm round cake pan.

    3.

  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.

    4.

  4. Fold in flour.

    5.

  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.

    6.

  6. Bake for 20 minute in the preheated oven, or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
